About Dr. Rajdeo

A Legacy of Skin Science & Aesthetic Mastery

Dr. Neetu Gehi Rajdeo is Navi Mumbai's foremost cosmetic dermatologist and trichologist, with over 20 years of dedicated expertise in skin, hair, and laser medicine. Trained at Lokmanya Tilak Municipal Medical College, Sion and INHS Asvini Naval Hospital, Mumbai, she combines rigorous clinical training with an artist's eye.

She has received specialized training in Botox & fillers from Dr. Susan Weinkle, USA, and mastered THERMAGE techniques from Australian experts. Her insights have been featured in The Deccan Chronicle, and she has demonstrated Botox techniques live on CNN. Her research has been presented at national and international dermatology conferences.
